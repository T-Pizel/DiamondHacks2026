UC Links

UC Links is a University of California network connecting university students with K-12 youth in
underserved communities. Through after-school programs, digital literacy workshops, and mentoring, UC
Links bridges the gap between higher education and the communities that need it most.

Why This Matters

In California, the communities most burdened by pollution are often the same ones with the
least access to quality education, technology, and support services. This isn't a coincidence —
it's a pattern rooted in decades of disinvestment.

CommPass exists to make the invisible visible. By mapping environmental data alongside
community resources, we empower residents to advocate for themselves and connect to the
support they deserve.

Where Do We Get Our Data?

Our environmental data comes from CalEnviroScreen 5.0, a tool developed by the California
Office of Environmental Health Hazard Assessment (OEHHA). It identifies communities most
affected by pollution and vulnerability using 21 indicators across pollution burden and population
characteristics.
